Words Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Local storage only

Words stores your saved word list locally on your device using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API. We do not operate a server and do not collect personal data.

Dictionary lookups

When you look up a word, the extension sends that word to dictionaryapi.dev to fetch definitions. That third-party service has its own privacy policy.

No sharing or selling

We do not sell, share, or transmit your saved word list to any party.

Deleting your data

Use Clear all in the extension popup, or uninstall the extension to remove all saved words from your device.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue at github.com/shoutideas/words.
