Words by Funthinkers.com — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Local storage only

Words stores your saved word list locally on your device using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API. We do not operate a server and do not collect personal data.

Dictionary lookups and pronunciation

When you look up a word, the extension sends that word to dictionaryapi.dev to fetch definitions. If you tap the speaker control, the extension may play pronunciation audio returned by that service. If audio is unavailable, your browser's built-in text-to-speech may be used instead. That third-party service has its own privacy policy.

No sharing or selling

We do not sell, share, or transmit your saved word list to any party.

Deleting your data

Remove individual saved words with the ✓ control in the lookup card or popup. To erase your entire saved word list, uninstall the extension from chrome://extensions.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue at github.com/shoutideas/words.
